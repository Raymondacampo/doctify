What is Doctify?

Doctify is your doctor search and appointment booking web app designed to simplify access to healthcare in the Dominican Republic. It provides a user-friendly platform for patients to find, review, and book appointments with verified doctors across various specialties. At the same time, it helps doctors reach more patients and manage their appointments efficiently.
