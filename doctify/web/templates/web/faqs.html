Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Doctify?

Doctify is a doctor search platform that helps you find and book appointments with the best doctors in the Dominican Republic. We aim to make healthcare accessible, modern, and user-friendly.

2. How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, simply create a patient account, search for a doctor based on your preferences, and click on the available time slot to confirm your booking.

3. Is Doctify free to use?

Yes, Doctify is free for patients to use. Doctors may have optional subscription plans for additional features, but basic registration is free.

4. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page. Choose whether you're a patient or a doctor and follow the registration process.

5. Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your dashboard. Simply find the appointment in your history and select the appropriate option.

6. How does Doctify ensure my data is secure?

We use advanced security measures, including Django's built-in security features, to ensure your personal and medical data is safe and confidential.

7. I’m a doctor. How can I list my services on Doctify?

If you’re a doctor, you can create an account and set up your profile by providing your credentials, specialties, and availability. Once verified, your profile will be visible to patients.
